    About Our Company

    Eviman Transport Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneering company shaping the future of sustainable mobility in Bharat. Incorporated on 30th June 2022 (CIN: U60232OR2022PTC040155), and proudly registered under UDYAM (MSME) – UDYAM-OD-25-0001667, we are also recognized by Startup India (DIPP106553) and Startup Odisha (OSP/SP/01471). Our journey is further supported through incubation at OUTR FINE, AIC Nalanda, and WRA Thane.

    XWe are the manufacturer of Bharat’s first Hybrid Quadricycle – a groundbreaking innovation that integrates Electric, Hydrogen, Solar, and Biofuel technologies. Designed with a dual powertrain system, our quadricycle offers a perfect balance of performance, sustainability, and affordability.

    At Eviman, safety is as important as innovation. That’s why we have developed the SCAB System– an advanced and affordable safety technology designed to make future transportation safer for everyone. Beyond mobility, we are also committed to empowering rural and semi-urban communities. Through our dedicated mobile app, we act as an aggregator of agricultural equipment, providing accessible, affordable, and efficient solutions that support India’s farming ecosystem.

    Our vision is rooted in the belief that transportation should not only be sustainable but also inclusive and affordable for all sections of society. As we look ahead, our next ambitious goal is to develop the most affordable hydrogen-powered transportation vehicle in the world – making clean mobility a reality for every Indian.
